How It Works

Step 1: Select Your Library

Open XtoCC and choose your Final Cut Pro X Event or Project. The app reads the fcpxml directly.

Step 2: Choose Destination

Pick your target—Premiere Pro, After Effects, Resolve, FCP 7, or others. XtoCC handles the conversion automatically.

Step 3: Review and Export

Preview the translated structure, then export the compatible XML for seamless import into your app.

Step 4: Import and Edit

Load the file in your destination software. Timelines, clips, and effects transfer intact.
